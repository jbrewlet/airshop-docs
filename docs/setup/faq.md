# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about AirShop.

## General

### What is AirShop?

AirShop is a platform for managing quotes, orders, and inventory for manufacturing and fabrication workflows.

### How do I get support?

Contact support through the app or visit [help.airshop.work](https://help.airshop.work) for contact options.

## Subscription

### How do I subscribe or change my plan?

Go to [Subscription](https://airshop.work/subscription) to subscribe or change your plan. Choose monthly or annual billing, select a plan, and complete checkout. To change an existing subscription (upgrade, downgrade, or cancel), use **Manage subscription** on the Subscription page. See [Subscription](subscription.md) for details.

### Who can manage subscription?

Only **Organization Administrators** can access the Subscription page and manage plans and payment. If you're not an admin, contact your organization admin.

### What happens if my trial ends or payment fails?

When your trial ends, you must subscribe to continue using AirShop. Go to [Subscription](https://airshop.work/subscription) to choose a plan. If a payment fails, you have a grace period to update your payment method. If not updated, access may be blocked until you reactivate. See [Subscription](subscription.md) for details.
