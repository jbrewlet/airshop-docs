# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is AirShop?

AirShop is a platform for managing quotes, orders, and inventory for manufacturing and fabrication workflows.

### How do I get support?

Contact support through the app or visit [help.airshop.work](https://help.airshop.work) for contact options.
