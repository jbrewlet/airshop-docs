# Quote Statuses

Statuses help your team quickly understand where each quote is in the customer journey.

[Open Quotes](https://airshop.work/quotes/all){ target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" }

---

## Common Statuses

| Status | What It Means | Notes |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **Draft** | Quote is being prepared and has not been sent to the customer. | Customer view tracking and follow-ups do not apply yet. |
| **Sent** | Quote has been sent and has an active public link for the customer. | Follow-ups and view tracking can apply once requirements are met. |
| **Viewed** | Customer has opened the quote link at least once. | View count and last viewed time are available. |

Depending on your workflow, you may also use additional labels. If your organization has custom conventions, align your team on what each label means.

---

## Why Status Matters

- **Follow-ups:** Follow-ups require quote status to be **SENT**. See [Quote Follow-ups](../follow-ups.md).
- **View Tracking:** Customer view metrics appear only for sent public quotes. See [Quote View Tracking](view-tracking.md).
- **Daily Prioritization:** Teams can focus first on drafts that need sending, then on sent quotes that have not been viewed.

---

## Recommended Team Workflow

1. Keep quotes in **Draft** while building and reviewing internally.
2. Send the quote to move it to **Sent**.
3. Watch for customer activity in [Quote View Tracking](view-tracking.md).
4. Use [Quote Follow-ups](../follow-ups.md) when reminders become due.
