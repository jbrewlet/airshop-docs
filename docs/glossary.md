# Glossary

Key terms you'll see in AirShop and these docs. Written in plain language, no jargon.

---

## Add New menu

Quick way to add an item, supplier, location, quote, or customer. Press `⌘;` (Mac) or `Ctrl+;` (Windows).

See [Keyboard Shortcuts](airshop/keyboard-shortcuts.md).

---

## AirShop

The app that helps you manage quotes, orders, and inventory for your shop or fabrication business.

See [Overview](getting-started/overview.md), [FAQ](setup/faq.md). [Open AirShop](https://airshop.work)

---

## Bin label

Small printed label for bins or shelves. Scan the QR code to open that item in AirShop.

See [Cards & Bin Labels](inventory/cards-and-labels.md), [QR Code Scanning](inventory/qr-code-scanning.md). [Open Bin Labels](https://airshop.work/bin-labels)

---

## Bulk import

Add or update many items at once by uploading a spreadsheet.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md). [Open Import](https://airshop.work/inventory/import)

---

## Cold storage

Where to store items that need refrigeration. Shown on Kanban cards.

See [Cards & Bin Labels](inventory/cards-and-labels.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## Dashboard

The main inventory screen when you open AirShop.

See [Quick Start](setup/quick-start.md). [Open Inventory](https://airshop.work/inventory)

---

## Embed

Code you paste into your website so the inquiry form appears right on the page.

See [Inquiry Forms](setup/inquiry-forms.md). [Open Forms](https://airshop.work/forms)

---

## Form drawer

The panel that opens when you click a form. Shows the link and embed code to share it.

See [Inquiry Forms](setup/inquiry-forms.md). [Open Forms](https://airshop.work/forms)

---

## Hosted URL

A link to your inquiry form. Share it in Instagram, email, or text. No website changes needed.

See [Inquiry Forms](setup/inquiry-forms.md). [Open Forms](https://airshop.work/forms)

---

## Inquiry

When someone fills out your form asking for a quote. You can turn it into a quote.

See [Inquiry Forms](setup/inquiry-forms.md), [Create a Quote](quotes/create-quote.md). [Open Inquiries](https://airshop.work/inquiries)

---

## Inventory item

A product or part you track: name, price, quantity, and where to buy it.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md), [Inventory Export](inventory/inventory-export.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## Item code

The short ID for each item (e.g. AC1F). When you scan a card, AirShop opens that item.

See [QR Code Scanning](inventory/qr-code-scanning.md). [Example item](https://airshop.work/inventory/AC1F)

---

## Kanban

A visual system for managing inventory and workflow. You use cards or signals to show what's in stock, what's running low, and when to reorder, so you only order what you need when you need it. Originally from Toyota and part of Lean Manufacturing.

See [Cards & Bin Labels](inventory/cards-and-labels.md), [QR Code Scanning](inventory/qr-code-scanning.md). [Open Cards](https://airshop.work/cards)

---

## Kanban card

Printed card (3" × 5") for your shop floor. Shows how much to order, low-stock alert, and lead time. Scan the QR to open the item.

See [Cards & Bin Labels](inventory/cards-and-labels.md), [QR Code Scanning](inventory/qr-code-scanning.md). [Open Cards](https://airshop.work/cards)

---

## Lead time

How many days until you receive an item after ordering it.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## Lean Manufacturing

An approach to making things with less waste: fewer defects, less waiting, less overstock. Focus on what the customer needs, when they need it. Kanban, just-in-time ordering, and continuous improvement are part of it.

See [Cards & Bin Labels](inventory/cards-and-labels.md). [Open Cards](https://airshop.work/cards)

---

## Location

Where you store an item: shelf, bin, or area.

See [Cards & Bin Labels](inventory/cards-and-labels.md). [Open Locations](https://airshop.work/inventory/locations)

---

## Low stock threshold

The quantity that triggers a "running low" alert.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## Organization

Your company or team's AirShop account. Admins manage settings and who can export or import.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md). [Open Organization Settings](https://airshop.work/settings/organization-settings)

---

## Purchase URL

The link to buy an item from your supplier. AirShop can clean up long links automatically.

See [URL Cleaning](inventory/url-cleaning.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## Quote

A proposal you send to a customer with prices and details. Create from an inquiry or from scratch.

See [Create a Quote](quotes/create-quote.md), [Can I just use quoting or inventory?](airshop/use-one-part.md). [Create Quote](https://airshop.work/quotes/create-quote), [Quotes](https://airshop.work/quotes)

---

## Quote Grid

Your list of inquiries. Click "Create Quote" next to any inquiry to turn it into a quote.

See [Create a Quote](quotes/create-quote.md). [Open Inquiries](https://airshop.work/inquiries)

---

## Reorder quantity

How many to order when you're running low. Shown as ORDER on Kanban cards.

See [Cards & Bin Labels](inventory/cards-and-labels.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## SKU

Your product or part number. Must be unique for each item.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## Source

A supplier or vendor you buy from. Link sources to items for quick reordering.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md). [Open Sources](https://airshop.work/sources)

---

## Status

Where an item stands: In stock, On order, Order now, Order soon, Researching, Archived, or Unknown.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md). [Open Items](https://airshop.work/inventory/items)

---

## Stocktake

A physical count of your inventory. AirShop helps you track what you counted vs. what the system shows.

See [Bulk Import](inventory/bulk-import.md), [Inventory Export](inventory/inventory-export.md). [Open Stocktake](https://airshop.work/stocktake)

---

## URL cleaning

AirShop automatically shortens and cleans up long product links when you paste them, so they stay reliable.

See [URL Cleaning](inventory/url-cleaning.md). [Open Inventory Settings](https://airshop.work/settings/inventory-settings)
